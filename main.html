RAWR / getrawr.com

Imagine that technology could help you stay close to your loved ones at all times! After two solid years of hard work our team is proud to present a revolutionary device. Meet RAWR TM - the smartest collar ever for our four-legged friends!
Being ardent pet-lovers and tech enthusiasts we created RAWR to help pet parents take the best care possible of their beloved furry family members. With RAWR you can stay tuned with your pet's life: keep them safe, healthy, fit and engaged from anywhere in the world.

THE CONCEPT

95% of pet owners say pets are family, and this is the reason we believe we're doing just the right thing helping people to stay connected to their furry loved ones at all times. Just imagine if every pet in the world had their own 24/7 watcher, veterinarian, fitness trainer and a personal...smartphone! No more lost, obese, unwell and upset ani-mals - RAWR smart collar is meant as a versatile solution for pet parents combining iconic design with absolute functionality.

And with a top-notch RAWR app all necessary features as well as reminders and alerts are right at hand.

We designed RAWR for modern pet parents who value sleek aesthetics along with proven functionality. Being curated to withstand the heaviest romping, RAWR is durable, shock- and waterproof. You can also customize your RAWR collar with an array of band colors and materials.

THE APP

Map

Locate your pet at no time with our advanced GPS technology. Or create safe zones for your pet and RAWR app will notify you if your escape artist wanders too far. Track and share your walks in just one click.

Monitor

Rest assured your furry friends are under control 24/7. Monitor vital health indicators and receive alerts if your pet is in pain or distress. Get detailed stats on pet's activity and calories expenditures to keep them in shape.

Train

You can even train your pet using special tutorials combined with the collar's vibro.

Menu

When you're on the go you want nothing more than a straightforward and user-friendly interface! We implemented convenient navigation allowing pet-parents to easily access an array of valuable functions from setting reminders to finding friends and locating pet-friendly spots in the neighbourhood.

THE HARDWARE

During the development process we made sure that only the latest technologies and the most advanced toolkits were applied. After many months of prototyping and testing we ended up with a neat device packed with powerful brain. With a blend of GPS, Bluetooth, cellular, Wi-Fi, 3-axis accelerometer and an array of sensors RAWR collar is capable of providing highly accurate results to let pet parent stay assured their furkids are safe and sound.
